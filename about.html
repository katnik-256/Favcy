Our Humble Beginnings

From a single act of kindness to a nationwide movement for children's rights and wellbeing

The Journey of FAVCY Child Care

FAVCY Child Care Uganda was born from the personal journey of our founder, Pastor Francis Opara—a story filled with hardship, faith, and the burning desire to make a difference in the lives of vulnerable children.

Raised in deep poverty where even the poor called him poor, Pastor Francis often went without food, missed school due to lack of fees, and faced daily struggles no child should ever endure. But those early challenges planted seeds of compassion and hope that would later blossom into FAVCY Child Care.

Working first as a teacher and later as a pastor in disadvantaged communities, he witnessed children going through the same suffering he had endured—hunger, lack of education, neglect, and being raised by elderly guardians with no means of support. The pain was familiar, and he knew he had to act.

What began in 2023 as small acts of kindness—providing meals, paying school fees for a few children, and offering counseling—has grown into an organization that reaches thousands of children across Uganda each year.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make and action we take

Child-Centered

Every decision we make prioritizes the best interests of the child. We listen to children and respect their rights, dignity, and potential.

Compassion

We serve with empathy and kindness, recognizing the inherent worth of every individual and treating all with respect and dignity.

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and accountability in all our actions and relationships.

Community

We believe in the power of community and work collaboratively with local stakeholders to create sustainable change.

Innovation

We embrace creative solutions and continuously seek to improve our approaches to better serve children and communities.

Faith

While serving all regardless of religion, our work is motivated by Christian values of love, hope, and service to others.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our growth and impact

2025

FAVCY is Born

Pastor Francis begins helping vulnerable children in his community, paying school fees for 5 children and providing basic necessities.

2024

Official Registration

FAVCY Child Care Uganda is officially registered as a nonprofit organization, expanding to reach 50 children across 3 communities.

2023

First Community Center

We establish our first community center in Kawempe, providing education support, meals, and counseling to 150 children weekly.

2019

Health Initiative Launch

We launch our mobile health clinic program, providing free medical checkups and treatment to over 500 children in underserved areas.

2021

COVID-19 Response

During the pandemic, we distribute food packages, learning materials, and hygiene kits to 1,200 vulnerable families.

2025

National Expansion

FAVCY expands operations to 4 new districts, reaching over 50 children annually with our programs.
