Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with FAVCY CHILD CARE?

We welcome volunteers in various capacities including teaching, healthcare, administration, and fieldwork. Please fill out our volunteer application form (link) or contact us directly to discuss opportunities.

What's the best way to make a donation?

You can make a one-time or recurring donation through our secure online portal, via mobile money (+256 740 391669), or by visiting our offices. All donations are tax-deductible.

Can I sponsor a specific child?

Yes! Our child sponsorship program allows you to support a specific child's education, healthcare, and nutrition needs. You'll receive regular updates about your sponsored child.

How can my organization partner with FAVCY?

We partner with corporations, schools, and other organizations in various ways. Please contact our partnerships team at partnerships@favcychildcare.org to discuss possibilities.

Do you accept in-kind donations?

Yes, we accept school supplies, medical equipment, clothing, and other items that support our programs. Please contact us first to coordinate donations.

About Us
