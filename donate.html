How Your Donation Helps

Education

50% of funds support our schools, teacher salaries, and learning materials to provide quality education to children who would otherwise go without.

Healthcare

30% funds our mobile clinics, nutrition programs, and medical treatments ensuring children grow up healthy and strong.

Child Protection

20% supports our child protection units, safe spaces, and counseling services for vulnerable children.
