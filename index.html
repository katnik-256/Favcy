About Us

Our Story

FAVCY Child Care Uganda was born from the personal journey of the founder, Pastor Francis Opara— a story filled with hardship, faith, and the desire to make a difference.

Raised in deep poverty where even the poor called him poor, Pastor Francis often went without food, missed school due to lack of fees, and faced daily struggles no child should ever endure. But those early challenges filled him with compassion and hope.

Working as teacher and Later, as a pastor in disadvantaged communities, he witnessed children going through the same suffering — hunger, lack of education, neglect, and being raised by elderly guardians with no support. He knew this pain personally — and he knew he had to do something.
