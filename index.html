About Us

FAVCY Child Care is a non-profit organization dedicated to the welfare of underprivileged children. Our mission is to provide holistic care including education, health, and emotional support, helping children to thrive and reach their full potential.

Our Services

  • Free tutoring and after-school programs
  • Nutrition and wellness support
  • Safe recreational activities
  • Family and counseling support
