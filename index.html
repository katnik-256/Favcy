About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2024, FAVCY CHILD CARE began as a small initiative to provide education to street children in Mumbai. Today, we operate in 12 states across India, reaching over 50,000 children annually.

Our mission is to ensure that every child, regardless of their background, has access to quality education, healthcare, and a safe environment to grow and thrive.

We believe that investing in children is the most effective way to break the cycle of poverty and create lasting change in communities.
