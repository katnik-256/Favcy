Program Details

Learn more about how each of our programs creates lasting change in children's lives

Education for All Program

Our Education for All initiative breaks down barriers to learning by providing comprehensive support to vulnerable children in Uganda.

Key Components:

  • School fee scholarships for orphans and vulnerable children
  • After-school tutoring and homework support
  • Provision of school supplies and uniforms
  • Digital literacy programs with computer labs
  • Teacher training and school infrastructure improvements
  • Early childhood development centers

Impact:

Last year, we supported 500+ children with education, achieving a 95% school attendance rate among our beneficiaries compared to the regional average of 65%.

Health & Nutrition Program

We address critical health needs through mobile clinics, nutrition programs, and health education.

Key Components:

  • Monthly mobile health clinics in rural areas
  • School feeding programs providing daily nutritious meals
  • Immunization drives and health screenings
  • Malnutrition treatment and prevention
  • HIV/AIDS education and support
  • Clean water and sanitation projects

Impact:

Our health interventions reached 5,000+ children last year, reducing malnutrition rates by 40% in communities we serve.

Child Protection Program

We create safe environments and provide critical support for children at risk of abuse, neglect, or exploitation.

Key Components:

  • Safe houses for emergency shelter
  • Counseling and trauma support services
  • Community child protection committees
  • Legal aid and advocacy for children's rights
  • Anti-trafficking awareness campaigns
  • Family reunification and foster care support

Impact:

Last year, we intervened in 300+ child protection cases and trained 50 community volunteers to identify and report abuse.

Vocational Training Program

We empower older youth with practical skills for self-sufficiency and economic independence.

Key Components:

  • 6-month intensive skills training programs
  • Courses in carpentry, tailoring, hairdressing, and more
  • Computer literacy and digital skills training
  • Business startup kits for graduates
  • Entrepreneurship and financial literacy training
  • Job placement assistance

Impact:

85% of our vocational graduates are employed or running their own businesses within 6 months of completing training.

Community Empowerment Program

We strengthen families and communities to better support children's development.

Key Components:

  • Parenting skills workshops
  • Income-generating activities for caregivers
  • Agricultural training and support
  • Community savings and loan associations
  • Gender equality and child rights education
  • Advocacy for child-friendly policies

Impact:

500 families increased their household income by an average of 30% through our empowerment programs last year.

Emergency Relief Program

We provide rapid response to children and families affected by crises.

Key Components:

  • Emergency food and water distribution
  • Temporary shelter and non-food items
  • Child-friendly spaces in disaster areas
  • Psychosocial support for trauma recovery
  • School reconstruction and supplies
  • Disaster preparedness training

Impact:

During recent floods, we provided emergency assistance to 1,200+ families, ensuring children's immediate needs were met.
